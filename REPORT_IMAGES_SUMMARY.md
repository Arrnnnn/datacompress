## üí° TIPS FOR CREATING DIAGRAMS

### Color Scheme (Consistent across all figures):

- **Input/Output:** Light Blue (#E3F2FD)
- **Processing:** Light Green (#E8F5E9)
- **Analysis:** Light Purple (#F3E5F5)
- **Compression:** Light Orange (#FFF3E0)
- **Encoding:** Light Yellow (#FFF9C4)
- **Lossy/Warning:** Light Red (#FFEBEE)

### Font Guidelines:

- **Title text:** Bold, 11-12pt
- **Body text:** Regular, 9-10pt
- **Labels:** Regular, 8-9pt
- **Font:** Arial or Calibri (for diagrams)

### Arrow Guidelines:

- **Main flow:** Thick (3-4pt), solid
- **Sub-processes:** Thin (2pt), solid
- **Optional paths:** Dashed
- **Color:** Black or dark gray
